Ham sandwhich

Fast and easy!

Ingredients

  • Two white bread slives
  • Thin cut deli ham
  • Slice of american cheese
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Lay out bread
  2. Apply ketchup to one side of a piece of bread
  3. lay cheese over ketchup, ham over cheese
  4. Lay second piece of bread on top of it all
