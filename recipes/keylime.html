Key lime pie

A amazing weekend treat!!

Key lime pie is an American dessert pie made of lime, egg yolks, and sweetened condensed milk. It may be served with no topping, topped with a meringue topping made from egg whites, or with whipped cream; it may be cooked in a pie crust, graham cracker crust, or no crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 eggs
  • >1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon grater lime
  • 9 inch pie crust
  • 6 tablespoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 425
  2. separate eggs
  3. Combine sugar,flour,cornstart (1cup)
  4. gradually stir into the egg yolks, stir in butter,lime juice and lime zest
  5. continue mixing until stiff and glossy
  6. Pour into pie shell, bake at 425 for 5-6 minutes
